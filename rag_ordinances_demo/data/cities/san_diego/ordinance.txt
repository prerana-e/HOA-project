CITY OF SAN DIEGO MUNICIPAL CODE
CHAPTER 14: GENERAL REGULATIONS
ARTICLE 2: BUILDING AND PROPERTY STANDARDS
DIVISION 5: FENCES, WALLS, AND HEDGES

§142.0501 PURPOSE
The purpose of this Division is to establish standards for fences, walls, and hedges to ensure public safety, preserve neighborhood character, and maintain clear sight lines at intersections.

§142.0510 DEFINITIONS
For purposes of this Division, the following definitions apply:
(a) "Fence" means a barrier constructed of wood, metal, vinyl, masonry, or other approved material.
(b) "Retaining wall" means a wall designed to resist lateral pressure of soil.
(c) "Sight visibility area" means the triangular area at street intersections as defined in Section 113.0273.

§142.0520 HEIGHT LIMITS - RESIDENTIAL ZONES
(a) FRONT YARD: Fences shall not exceed forty-two (42) inches in height within required front yard setbacks.
(b) SIDE YARD: Fences may be up to six (6) feet in height in side yards behind the front building line.
(c) REAR YARD: Fences may be up to six (6) feet in height in rear yards.
(d) CORNER LOTS: Within the sight visibility area, fences shall not exceed thirty-six (36) inches in height.
(e) EXCEPTION: An additional two (2) feet of open lattice or similar material with at least fifty percent (50%) open area may be added above the maximum heights.

§142.0530 MATERIALS AND CONSTRUCTION
(a) PERMITTED MATERIALS: Wood, vinyl, wrought iron, chain link, concrete block, and decorative metal are permitted.
(b) PROHIBITED MATERIALS: Barbed wire, razor wire, electrically charged fencing, and sheet metal are prohibited in residential zones.
(c) STRUCTURAL REQUIREMENTS: All fences over four (4) feet shall be designed to withstand wind loads per Building Code Section 1609.

§142.0540 PERMIT REQUIREMENTS
(a) PERMIT REQUIRED: A building permit is required for:
    (1) Any fence over six (6) feet in height
    (2) Masonry or concrete fences over three (3) feet in height
    (3) Fences with electrical components
(b) PERMIT EXEMPT: Fences six (6) feet or less in height constructed of wood, vinyl, or chain link are exempt from permit requirements if located in rear or side yards.
(c) FEES: Fence permit fees are established in the City's fee schedule.

§142.0550 SETBACK REQUIREMENTS
(a) Fences may be constructed on property lines with neighbor consent.
(b) Fences shall maintain a minimum three (3) foot setback from public sidewalks on corner lots.
(c) Fences shall not encroach upon utility easements or obstruct access to meters.

§142.0560 MAINTENANCE STANDARDS
(a) All fences shall be maintained in a structurally sound and aesthetically acceptable condition.
(b) The finished side of a fence shall face neighboring properties and public rights-of-way.
(c) Damaged fences shall be repaired within thirty (30) days of written notice from the City.

§142.0570 ENFORCEMENT AND PENALTIES
Violations of this Division are subject to administrative citations as follows:
(a) First violation: Warning notice
(b) Second violation: $100 fine
(c) Third and subsequent violations: $250 fine per occurrence
