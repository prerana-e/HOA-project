LOS ANGELES MUNICIPAL CODE
CHAPTER I: GENERAL PROVISIONS AND ZONING
ARTICLE 2: SPECIFIC PLANNING - RESIDENTIAL ZONES
SECTION 12.22: FENCE AND WALL REGULATIONS

SEC. 12.22.A.1 INTENT AND PURPOSE
This Section regulates the construction of fences and walls to ensure safety, privacy, and aesthetic compatibility within residential neighborhoods of Los Angeles.

SEC. 12.22.A.2 DEFINITIONS
As used in this Section:
(a) "Fence" shall mean any structure serving as an enclosure, barrier, or boundary.
(b) "Wall" shall mean a solid barrier constructed of masonry, concrete, or similar materials.
(c) "Required yard" shall mean the minimum setback area required by zoning regulations.

SEC. 12.22.A.3 HEIGHT REGULATIONS - SINGLE FAMILY ZONES
(a) FRONT YARDS: Maximum height of three and one-half (3.5) feet.
(b) SIDE YARDS (street side of corner lot): Maximum height of three and one-half (3.5) feet within the required front yard setback area.
(c) SIDE YARDS (interior): Maximum height of six (6) feet.
(d) REAR YARDS: Maximum height of six (6) feet.
(e) VISIBILITY TRIANGLE: Within ten (10) feet of any driveway or street intersection, fences shall not exceed three (3) feet in height.

SEC. 12.22.A.4 HEIGHT REGULATIONS - MULTI-FAMILY ZONES
(a) FRONT YARDS: Maximum height of three and one-half (3.5) feet, or six (6) feet if setback at least five (5) feet from the property line.
(b) SIDE AND REAR YARDS: Maximum height of eight (8) feet.
(c) Security fences up to ten (10) feet may be permitted with a Zoning Administrator approval.

SEC. 12.22.A.5 MATERIALS STANDARDS
(a) PERMITTED: Wood, vinyl, wrought iron, decorative block, chain link (rear yards only), and tubular steel.
(b) PROHIBITED: Barbed wire in residential zones (except industrial-adjacent properties), electrified fencing, plywood, and corrugated metal.
(c) CHAIN LINK: May only be used in rear yards and must include privacy slats if visible from public right-of-way.

SEC. 12.22.A.6 PERMIT REQUIREMENTS
(a) BUILDING PERMIT REQUIRED for:
    (1) Any fence or wall exceeding six (6) feet in height
    (2) All masonry walls exceeding three (3) feet in height
    (3) Retaining walls exceeding three (3) feet in exposed height
(b) PERMIT EXEMPTION: Wood or vinyl fences six (6) feet or less in side or rear yards.
(c) PERMIT FEE: $92.00 base fee plus inspection fees per current fee schedule.

SEC. 12.22.A.7 SWIMMING POOL ENCLOSURES
(a) All swimming pools shall be enclosed by a fence or wall at least five (5) feet in height.
(b) Gates shall be self-closing and self-latching with latches at least fifty-four (54) inches above grade.
(c) Fence openings shall not permit passage of a four (4) inch sphere.

SEC. 12.22.A.8 ENFORCEMENT
Violations of this Section shall be processed as Building Code violations subject to:
(a) Notice to comply
(b) Administrative fines up to $500 per day
(c) Mandatory removal of non-compliant structures
